About us

JN is a export company, which procures and supplies coir substrates in keeping with our values. We hold valid trading license for coir exports in India and Overseas.

Our services include manufacturing and exporting Coir fibers and Coco peats to China and European Countries from India. We focus on continuing to offer dedicated services to our customers with traditional integrity and trustworthiness.
